About Our Mosque

Welcome to Al-Noor Mosque

Established in 1995, Al-Noor Mosque has been serving the Muslim community in New York for over 25 years. Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for worship, education, and community building.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of Islamic knowledge, spirituality, and service that enriches individuals and society.
