About Our Mosque

Since 1985

Serving the Muslim community

Welcome to Al-Noor Mosque

Al-Noor Mosque was established in 1985 to serve the growing Muslim community in our city. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant Islamic center offering daily prayers, Quran classes, Islamic education, and community services.

Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for worship, learning, and community building. We strive to promote understanding of Islam and foster good relations with all people regardless of faith or background.
